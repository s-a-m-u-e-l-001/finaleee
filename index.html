Frequently Asked Questions

What health guarantees do you provide? +
All our puppies come with a comprehensive health guarantee covering genetic conditions for 2 years. We provide vaccination records, health certificates, and 30-day health insurance coverage.
How much does delivery cost within Nairobi? +
Delivery within Nairobi is KSH 2,000. For other counties, delivery ranges from KSH 5,000 to KSH 8,000 depending on the distance. We ensure safe, comfortable transport for your new puppy.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. A 50% deposit is required to reserve your puppy, with the balance due at pickup or delivery.
Do you provide training support? +
Yes! We offer comprehensive training guides and 24/7 phone support for the first 3 months. We also have professional trainers available for in-home sessions at KSH 3,500 per session.
Can I visit and see the puppies before buying? +
Absolutely! We encourage visits to our facility in Karen, Nairobi. You can meet the puppies, see their living conditions, and even meet the parent dogs. Please call ahead to schedule your visit.
